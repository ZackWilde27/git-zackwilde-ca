ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Talbot Grimes, but everyone just calls me The Blight. Ever since I was a kid, I've been fascinated with how even the smallest of plants could have serious health effects from the positive to the negative, and dedicated my life to biochemisty. After contributing to science I have spent time studying the art of running very fast, but only in a straight line directly into a wall. These days I enjoy a nice nightly stroll in the woods where I sacrifice survivors to The Entity in a death game. Some people have told me that I should find a more normal hobby, like knitting, but I just can't see myself doing anything else.

I've been sacrificing survivors to the entity since 2020. Always in a rush, I'm a busy guy.
