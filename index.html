Bio

Me

I love creative projects and logical problem-solving, taking established concepts and thinking outside of the box to solve problems. As an 8 year old I was teaching myself Microsoft Word with the hopes of one day becoming a writer, but nowadays I enjoy IT and Computer Assisted Design projects.



My Music

I make intense dubstep, and the way I'd describe my style is a rhythm-over-pattern approach. The call is always part of the established pattern but the response to the call usually isn't. It's possible to tell where in the drop a given section is, because of the unique patterns throughout.

My Game Philosophy

I started game development mainly for one reason: When I've perfected my craft enough to ship games I am hoping to change the industry. The problem with modern video games is all the cool stuff we lost around the mid 2000s. I want to bring back cheat codes, glitches that improve user experience, and generally allowing the player to make their own fun if that's what they want.



Other Achievements

I branch out as much as possible because I love seeing what's possible on my own systems. I run my website off my own server because it was fun to learn, and I want to see what's possible.
